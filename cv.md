## Numerical Experience

Oliver et al. (2023) and Calkins et al. (in prep) used and modified the spectral solver QuICC (Marti et al. 2016), which handles planar and spherical geometries. Implemented new model to handle internal heating for rotating Boussinesq convection.

Oliver et al. (2025), Oliver et al. (in prep), and Calkins et al. (in prep) used the spectral element code Nek5000 (Fischer et al. 2019) which handles arbitrary geometry.

- Rayleigh Hackathon, Golden, CO (2023)
- Nek5000 User Workshop, Richmond, VA (2025)
- Familiarity with spectral/pseudo-spectral codes: Dedalus, Rayleigh, and Coral
- Proficient in Python, Fortran, and MATLAB
